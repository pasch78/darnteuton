How to Play

Your goal is simple: maximize your score before the 5-minute timer runs out. You earn points by unscrambling the board and finding valid 5-letter words.

Grid Mechanics

  • The Frame: The first and last letters of every row are locked. You must type 5-letter dictionary words that start and end with those exact letters.
  • Direct Typing: Use the on-screen or physical keyboard. Your guess will automatically mirror across all valid rows that match the first letter you type.
  • Row Indicators: The fraction (e.g., 0/12) shows how many valid words you have found out of the total possibilities for that row.
  • Linked Rows (🔗): If rows share identical start and end letters, they link. Finding a word here populates both rows simultaneously for double the points!
  • Dead Rows: Grayed-out rows mean zero valid 5-letter words exist in the dictionary for that letter combination. They are disabled so you don't waste time.

The Hint Engine

  • Cascading Hints: Every correct guess triggers the hint engine. The middle letters of your guess will sweep the board. If any unfound words share those letters in the exact same positions, they are revealed as free hints!
  • Chain Reactions: Hints update dynamically. Unlocking one word usually gives you the missing piece to unlock another.

Scoring & Bonuses

The game uses a master dictionary of roughly 13,000 words. About 5,700 are considered "common," while the rest are "obscure."

  • Rarity Scaling (Base Score): Rows with fewer hidden solutions yield massive payouts, while common rows offer smaller rewards. A row with 200 possible words might award 5 points per guess, while a tough row with only 20 options awards 50 points.
  • Obscure Words (+50 points): Impress the engine by submitting a rare vocabulary word for an immediate 50-point bonus.
  • Speed Streak (+5 points): Submit 3 valid words quickly (within 15 seconds of each other) to catch fire. While the streak is active, every fast guess earns bonus points. Warning: Typing an invalid word instantly breaks your streak!

Winning Tips

  • Fish for Hints: Don't get stuck staring at a hard row. Guess common words in easy rows to spawn helpful hints across the rest of the board.
  • Target Rare Rows: Check the row counters immediately at launch. Prioritize rows with tiny word pools (e.g., 0/2 or 0/5) because they offer the largest base point infusions.
  • Protect Your Streak: When your Speed Streak is active, don't guess blindly. If you are running out of time to keep the streak alive, quickly solve a high-volume, easy row.

About the Game

Inspired by "Gefüllte Kalbsbrust"
5 IN 5 IN 5 is a digital reimagining of a classic German pen-and-paper word game called Gefüllte Kalbsbrust (which translates directly to "Stuffed Breast of Veal").

In the original parlor game, a single long word is written down the left side of a page, and then written backwards up the right side. Players then race to "stuff" the inside of the word by filling the space between the outer letters to create new, valid words. We took that elegant mechanical "frame" and cranked up the speed.
