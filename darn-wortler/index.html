5 IN 5 IN 5

Your daily word sprint or zen practice.

How to Play
⏱️

The Game: 5 rows. 5 letters each. Find words before the timer runs out, or play untimed in Zen Mode.

🔠

The Grid: Outer letters are locked. Guess 5-letter words starting and ending with those exact letters.

💡💡

Cascade Hints: Correct guesses reveal bonus letters across the board. Use them to your advantage.

How to Score
+50 pts

Bounty Badges: Harder rows are worth more points per valid guess. Green badges are easy, red are hard!

✦

Rare Words: Bonus points for obscure vocabulary, plus massive base points in black Bonus Rows.

🔥

Speed Streak: (Timed Mode Only) 3 fast answers catch fire. Keep the streak alive for bonus points.
