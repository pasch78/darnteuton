Darn Wortler

Inspired by the German word game "Gefüllte Kalbsbrust"

The Objective
⏱️

Score as many points as possible before the 5-minute timer expires.

How to Play
🌱

Top Row: A random five-letter seed word for this puzzle round.

🥪

Game Grid: Guess five-letter words that fit between the given outer letters. The left column is your seed word; the right column is the seed word in reverse.

📊

Row Difficulty: Some rows are much tougher than others with few valid solutions. The tougher the row, the higher the score!

Bonus Points
✨

Great Vocabulary: Earn +50 points for each obscure but valid word.

🔥

Speed Streak: Type 3 valid words within 15s to unlock a persistent +5 point bonus per answer.
