Welcome to Darn Wortler

Inspired by the classic German word game "Gefüllte Kalbsbrust", Darn Wortler is a high-speed, 5-minute race against the clock. Unscramble the game board and see how high you can push your score before time runs out!

How to Play
🧩

The Grid: The seed word at the top of the board creates the frame for the game. You must guess 5-letter words that begin and end with the outer letters shown in the rows below.

⌨️

Type & Submit: Use the on-screen keyboard or your device's keyboard to type your guess and press Enter. The game ends when the 5-minute timer runs out.

👻

Cascade Hints: Every correct guess triggers the hint engine. The middle letters of your guess will sweep the board, revealing hints for words you haven't found yet.

How to Score
📈

Rarity Pays: The fewer valid dictionary words there are for a row, the more points you earn for finding one.

✨

Obscure Vocabulary: Impress the engine with a rare word to score a +50 point bonus!

🔥

Catch Fire: Submit 3 correct words in rapid succession to activate the Speed Streak. Every subsequent fast guess earns bonus points, but an invalid guess will break your streak!
